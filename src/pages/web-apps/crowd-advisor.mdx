As part of [HackUTD VII](https://hackutd-viii.devpost.com/), I created [CrowdAdvisor](https://financial-analyzer.web.app/) with 3 other group members. CrowdAdvisor presents social media sentiment, most popular news, tweets, and financial information to the user in order to make more informed decisions about investing in stocks and cryptocurrency.
